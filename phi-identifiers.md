### HIPAA 18 Identifiers for Protected Health Information (PHI)

1. Name
2. Address (all geographic subdivisions smaller than state)
3. All elements (except years) of dates related to an individual
4. Telephone numbers
5. Fax number
6. Email address
7. Social Security Number
8. Medical record number
9. Health plan beneficiary number
10. Account number
11. Certificate or license number
12. Vehicle identifiers and serial numbers, including license plate numbers
13. Device identifiers and serial numbers
14. Web URL
15. Internet Protocol (IP) Address
16. Finger or voice print
17. Photographic image
18. Any other characteristic that could uniquely identify the individual
